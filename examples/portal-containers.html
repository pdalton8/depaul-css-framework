One University With Unlimited Opportunity

A single application to DePaul opens the door to hundreds of programs, world-class faculty and unlimited opportunities to build your skills and brighten your future. Whether you're exploring your passions, shaping your career path or connecting with mentors, DePaul's unique academic approach is designed to support every student's growth and success during their time here.

  • Layered-Learning™

    At DePaul, our unique Layered-Learning™ approach brings our Vincentian values to life by combining classroom lessons with project-based and community-based service learning.

  • Classroom Learning

    With a 17:1 student-to-faculty ratio, classroom learning is not a distant lecture but a living dialogue, shaped by the spirit of Vincentian personalism.

  • Project Based Learning

    Here at DePaul, we challenge students to learn by doing, standing shoulder to shoulder with faculty who have spent decades building premier partnerships across Chicago and globally.

  • Community Engagement

    Guided by our Vincentian mission, DePaul students engage with Chicago's diverse communities, turning social justice from an ideal into lived experience.

  • Research & Innovation

    From undergraduate research opportunities to cutting-edge labs, DePaul provides students the resources and mentorship to explore, discover and innovate.

Usage

Copy the HTML for any container above and paste it into your portal page. The containers are fully responsive and styled using the DePaul Bootstrap Theme.

Available Variants

  • Feature Container
    • .feature-container - Default (image on left)
    • .feature-container--image-right - Image on right
  • Quote Banner
    • .quote-banner - Default (image on left)
    • .quote-banner--image-right - Image on right
  • Background Image + CTA
    • .bg-image-cta - Full-width background with blue overlay and semi-transparent white card
    • .bg-image-cta--no-overlay - Full-width background with no overlay and solid white card with shadow
    • .bg-image-cta--image-right - Content card aligned to the right
    • Perfect for call-to-action sections, program finders, or announcements
  • Three Card Section
    • .three-card-section - Flexible card grid with 3 cards in responsive layout
    • Includes header with title and description
    • Cards feature eyebrow text, title, description, and action button
    • Perfect for events, services, features, or any three-item showcase
  • Event Schedule
    • .event-schedule - Timeline-style schedule display for conferences or day-of events
    • Vertical timeline with time markers and connecting line
    • Event cards include title, speaker/location metadata, description, and optional tags
    • Fully responsive - adapts timeline layout for mobile devices

Add Background Color

Add utility classes like .bg-light or .bg-primary to the section for different backgrounds.
